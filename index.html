A "To-Do-List" for movies, often called a movie bucket list.

Franchise Films: For fans of certain franchises (e.g., Star Wars, Harry Potter), the list might comprise all the movies within that series or universe.

Cult Favorites: Some to-do lists include cult films or hidden gems that have gained a dedicated fan following over the years. These movies often have quirky or unconventional elements that make them stand out.

Foreign Films: To broaden one's cinematic horizons, foreign films with subtitles may be included. This can be an opportunity to experience different cultures and storytelling traditions.
